Who Are We?

We are second-year students from Cotabato State University, and we are proud to present ourselves not just as learners, but as young innovators stepping into the world of digital commerce and technology. Today, we stand before you as a team committed to bridging the gap between traditional local businesses and the growing online market. Our project focuses on a beloved local store from Cotabato City — MAYAN STORE — a trusted name in the community that deserves to thrive in the digital age. As students, we are continuously learning. But through this project, we go beyond theory. We are applying real skills in web development, e-commerce systems, user interface design, and customer experience. This isn't just about building a website — it’s about helping a local business grow, reach more customers, and stay relevant in a fast-evolving digital world.
